Erik Bloomquist is an All-State saxophone player who has studied with Pittsburgh jazz legend Greg Grenek and Mr. Charles Pillow, professor of jazz saxophone at Eastman School of Music. He has experience playing in various settings, including big bands, combos, orchestras, and studio recording. His favorite saxophone players are David Sanborn and Charlie Parker. His favorite song to play with the band is Retro-Spect.
